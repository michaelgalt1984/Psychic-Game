The Psychic Game

Guess which letter I am thinking of... You guess wrong, you lose! You guess right... then I guess that you are psychic!

The English alphabet has 26 letters and you'll get 3 guesses to guess the letter. The only options are letters, so numbers or special characters are not valid guesses. If you can't figure it out with 3 guesses, you obviously aren't psychic and just got lucky ;)
